Marine Sentinel Solutions Our team of six CS students is developing an innovative system to reduce oil spills and provide real-time alerts. As part of the Smart India Hackathon, we're tackling environmental challenges caused by oil spills. In 2023, about 2,000 metric tons of oil were spilled. Major disasters, like the 2010 Deepwater Horizon, killed thousands of marine animals. Our system can monitor Mumbai Port's 400-square-kilometer harbor in real time, helping prevent future spills and protect marine life. For more information or to support our mission, feel free to contact us.
